### How It Will Work

<Steps>
  <Step title="Email Account Setup">
    Connect your email account securely using OAuth authentication.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Automatic Processing">
    AI will scan emails for receipts and bills automatically.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Data Extraction">
    Extract amounts, dates, merchants, and due dates from emails.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Transaction Creation">
    Automatically create transactions in your AI2Fin account.
  </Step>
</Steps>
