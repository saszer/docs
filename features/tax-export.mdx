## How to Access Tax Export

### Primary Navigation Path

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Sidebar Navigation">

  <Step title="Navigate to Reports and Trends">

## Tax Export Features

### Creating Tax Exports

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Export Period">

    3. Include/exclude specific dates
    4. Preview transaction count
  </Step>
