## Welcome to AI2Fin

Get up and running with AI2Fin's AI-powered financial management platform in just a few minutes. This guide will walk you through the essential steps to start managing your finances intelligently.

<Info>
**Prerequisites:** You'll need bank statements or transaction data in CSV format, and basic business information for tax settings.
</Info>

After completing these steps, continue with [User Preferences & Settings](/features/user-preferences) to fine-tune GST rates and AI behavior.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

### Sign Up Process

1. **Visit the Platform**
   - Go to [app.ai2fin.com](https://app.ai2fin.com)
   - Click "Get Started" or "Sign Up"

2. **Account Setup**
   - Enter your email address
   - Create a secure password
   - Verify your email address

3. **Business Information**
   - Company name and type
   - Business registration number
   - Primary business address
   - Tax year settings

<Card title="Pro Tip" icon="lightbulb">
Set up your account with accurate business information for better AI categorization and tax optimization.
</Card>

## Step 2: Import Your Financial Data

### CSV Import Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Prepare Your Data">
    Export your bank statements from your banking platform in CSV format. Most banks support this export feature.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Upload to AI2Fin">
    1. Go to the Dashboard
    2. Click "Upload CSV" or "Import Data"
    3. Select your CSV file
    4. Confirm the upload
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Data Processing">
    AI2Fin will automatically process and categorize your transactions using AI algorithms.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Supported CSV Formats

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Common Bank Formats">
    - **Standard CSV**: Most banks worldwide support CSV export
    - **PDF Statements**: Universal bank statement format
    - **Custom Formats**: Flexible import mapping for any bank
    - **Multi-Currency**: Support for international transactions
    - **Accounting Software**: QuickBooks, Xero, and other exports
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Required CSV Columns">
    - **Date**: Transaction date (DD/MM/YYYY format)
    - **Description**: Transaction description
    - **Amount**: Transaction amount (positive for income, negative for expenses)
    - **Balance**: Account balance (optional)
    - **Reference**: Transaction reference number (optional)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Receipt Capture (Mobile & PDF)

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Snap or Upload" icon="camera">
    - Tap the camera icon in chat to photograph a receipt on your phone
    - Upload images or PDFs (JPG, PNG, PDF, HEIC) through the chat attachment button
    - Store the asset securely for linking to transactions or expenses later
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI Extraction" icon="robot">
    - The assistant reads merchant, date, total, and GST from every file
    - Matches receipts to transactions within ±7 days and ±1% amount tolerance
    - Creates a new expense automatically when no match exists
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Step 4: Set Up Tax Preferences

### GST Configuration

<Card title="GST Settings" icon="receipt">
Configure your GST preferences for accurate tax calculations and reporting.
</Card>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable GST">
    1. Go to Settings → Tax Preferences
    2. Toggle "Enable GST"
    3. Set your GST registration status
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Rates">
    - Default GST rate: 10%
    - Custom rates for specific categories
    - Zero-rated items configuration
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Business Use Percentage">
    Set default business use percentages for different expense types.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Tax Deduction Settings

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Common Deductible Expenses">

## FAQs

### What should I configure first for GST compliance?
Complete the Country & Tax preferences so AI2Fin applies the correct GST/VAT rate, tax terminology, and receipt requirements from day one.

### How do I digitize receipts during onboarding?
Use the chat camera or attachment button to upload receipts as you review transactions. AI2Fin extracts the data and links each file to the right expense automatically.

### Can I invite teammates during setup?
Yes. Once your workspace is configured, invite teammates so they inherit the same tax rules, categories, and AI configurations while keeping individualized preferences.
